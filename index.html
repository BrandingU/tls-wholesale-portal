Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a TLS wholesale partner.

Entry Tier partners require a minimum monthly order of $1,000. Preferred Tier partners require a minimum of $1,700 per month. These minimums ensure you maintain your wholesale pricing and partner status. Orders can include any combination of TLS products.
We offer net-30 terms for established Preferred Tier partners with approved credit. New partners typically pay upfront via credit card or ACH. We also accept payment through major financing options for larger orders. Contact your account manager to discuss payment arrangements.
All wholesale orders ship free within the continental United States. International shipping is available with competitive rates. Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days, with most domestic deliveries arriving within 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available for urgent orders.
Preferred Tier partners enjoy a 30-day return policy on unopened, resalable inventory. Entry Tier partners have a 15-day window. This policy gives you flexibility to test products in your market without excessive risk. Contact support to initiate a return authorization.

Preferred Tier partners also get live training sessions with our product specialists and priority invitations to exclusive educational events.
