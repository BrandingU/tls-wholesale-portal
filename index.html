Why Build a Partnership with TLS?

We're not just selling products — we're building a movement.

The TLS wholesale program was designed to create genuine partnerships with businesses that share our mission: helping more people experience the benefits of scalar wave technology. We choose our partners carefully because we believe the right fit benefits everyone — your business, your clients, and the broader TLS community.

Unlike typical wholesale programs that treat you as just another account number, TLS views wholesale partners as an extension of our team. When you succeed, we succeed. That's why we've built flexible minimums, tiered pricing, and responsive support into the foundation of this program.

Supplement category — We offer a 10–25% discount on supplements, separate from our main 40–55% device discount. This keeps supplement margins competitive while maintaining the integrity of our core pricing structure.

High-ticket category minimums — For CUBE, SPHERE, PYRAMID, and CHAIR, we require category-level minimums (not per-unit minimums). This protects partner margins and keeps distribution healthy as the program scales.

Custom large-order pricing — For partners with orders exceeding standard tier maximums, we offer custom pricing on a case-by-case basis. Contact your account specialist to discuss volume-based arrangements.
